Tracking Crime and Punishment in the United States

Project Description: This project seeks to create a visual representation of crime data within the United States given both federal and state data.

Our Macro summary gives basic information about each state's prison population relative to the others, while our microsummaries provide more information about crime and incarceration rates over the years for each state. The data for each state is compared to the federal data.
